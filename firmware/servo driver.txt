servo driver

init - turn stuff on and init them

calibrate_bare_servo - find out min and max PWM for servo when not installed

calibrate_installed_servo - find out where the walls are

get_max_position - get the highest value to send to servo class (0 to max)

start_move - takes position as argument

is_moving - return true or false (failsafe - WDT - reboot device when servo stuck)
